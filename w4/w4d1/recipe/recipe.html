Cheggy's Easy Eggycake Recipe

INGREDIENTS

  • 6 eggs
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 tsp salt

DESCRIPTION

Cheggy loves his eggs. This week, we will be making eggcake, a soft, airy, cake made from eggs, and 4 other common ingredients.

DIRECTIONS

  1. Mix em all up in a bowl with a whisk
  2. Line cupcake pans with cupcake paper
  3. Pour into each cup until half full
  4. Bake for 30 min at 350F
  5. Let cool for 15 min before enjoying!
